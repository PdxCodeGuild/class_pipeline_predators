The Bio of Ichigo Ichigo_Kurosaki

Ichigo is a popular anime character from the popular anime series Bleach. He's best known for being a rebellious kind hearted character whose temper is known to get the best of him. Despite the short temper, his name has a meaning: "to protect one thing".
